How to Combine Multiple PDFs into One Document

Merging multiple PDF files into a single, cohesive document has never been easier or more secure. Whether you're combining invoices, attaching supporting portfolios to a resume, or collating research papers, ToolNest does it instantly in a few clicks.

  1. Select multiple PDF files at once from the upload box above, or use the "Add More" button to append files one by one into your list.
  2. Review the list to ensure all your selected files are correct and in the right order.
  3. Click "Merge PDFs". Our fast local processor will seamlessly stitch them together into a brand new PDF ready for download.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is it safe to merge confidential documents?

Yes 100%. Our merging tool runs securely right inside your web browser using HTML5 technology. This guarantees that your private financial, legal, or personal invoices are never copied to an external or third-party server.

2. How many PDFs can I merge together?

There are absolutely no generic limits! You can keep adding dozens of documents seamlessly. The only restriction is the memory hardware belonging to your current device, as all file stitching operates locally.

3. Do I use up internet data combining files?

No! Because our tool completely processes your request on your own machine without uploading them to cloud servers, you save 100% of the internet bandwidth! A truly offline-grade integration on the web.
