Frequently Asked Questions

How does the background remover work?
Our tool uses color-based edge detection algorithms to identify and remove the background from your image. It works best with high-contrast images where the subject clearly stands out from the background.
What format is the output?
The output is always a PNG file, which supports transparency. This means you get a clean cutout with no background.
Is there a watermark on the result?
No. ToolzNest never adds watermarks to any output. Your images are yours.
Does it work with all images?
It works best with images where the subject has clear edges and good contrast with the background. Complex scenes with similar colors may need manual adjustment of the sensitivity slider.
