How to Shrink Image Size to Exact Custom Limits

Many websites, government portals, and university application forms reject high-resolution smartphone photos explicitly because they exceed strict upload caps like "Max Size: 100KB". Our powerful compressor intelligently crushes your file size down to the precise kilobyte limit you require.

  1. Select your heavy photograph or scan from your device securely.
  2. In the Target Size box, enter specific targets such as "50" and select "KB".
  3. Tap Compress. Our algorithm runs a highly sophisticated "Binary Search" to elegantly reduce the image step-by-step until it perfectly sneaks below your designated size limit.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How is this tool different from generic compressors?

Basic compressors offer "High/Medium/Low" sliders that force you to endlessly guess and download repeatedly. ToolNest actually accepts exact byte-size inputs, guaranteeing success on your first upload to strict platforms.

Is this offline and safe?

Yes. Our architecture processes the entirety of the file-size optimization using your computer's own memory rather than a remote cloud server.
